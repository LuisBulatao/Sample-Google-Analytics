PHP



PHP (Hypertext Preprocessor) is a widely-used open-source scripting language designed primarily for web development. It runs on the server side and is especially suited for creating dynamic and interactive websites. PHP integrates easily with databases like MySQL and supports various frameworks such as Laravel and CodeIgniter, which simplify the development process. Its syntax is simple for beginners but powerful enough for professionals to build robust applications. PHP powers major content management systems like WordPress, Joomla, and Drupal. Despite evolving competition, PHP continues to thrive due to its versatility, ease of deployment, and strong community support. From small blogs to complex enterprise applications, PHP remains a reliable solution for server-side scripting.
